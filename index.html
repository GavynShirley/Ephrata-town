Ephrata, Pennsylvania

Explore

Fairs

Celebrate year-round with various fairs and festivals, Whether it's the joy of the holidays or in the spirit of summer festivities, there's always a reason to come together in this town's lively atmosphere!

The Amish

Step into the past in a community rooted in 18th-century traditions. Experience horse-drawn buggies and explore farms supplying fresh produce, showcasing a lifestyle that endures through time.

The Holidays

Immerse yourself in the holiday spirit as you drive through Main Street, captivated by the enchanting holiday lights, festive decorations, and a grand Christmas tree at the town center.
